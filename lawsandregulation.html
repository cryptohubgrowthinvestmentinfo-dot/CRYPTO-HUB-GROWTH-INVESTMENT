Every user must read and accept these laws before registration is considered complete. Acceptance is securely stored in real-time database.

1. Account Registration Compliance

All users must provide accurate legal identity, active contact information, and valid payment details. False identities, duplicate registrations, impersonation, or unauthorized access attempts may lead to immediate suspension or permanent account closure.

2. Deposit & Funding Rules

  • All deposits must be submitted through officially approved company payment channels.
  • Deposits are only credited after internal verification and database confirmation.
  • Fraudulent payment reversals, chargebacks, or fake confirmations may trigger legal review and account restriction.

3. Investment Participation Terms

  • Investment plans operate according to selected plan terms and maturity timelines.
  • Users acknowledge market, operational, and liquidity conditions attached to investment participation.
  • Profits, withdrawals, and reinvestment options are governed by active company policy and internal verification systems.

4. Withdrawal & Security Policy

  • Withdrawals may require account verification, anti-fraud checks, and internal approval.
  • Suspicious transactions may be paused for compliance review.
  • Users are responsible for safeguarding passwords and device access.

5. Anti-Fraud & Misconduct

Any manipulation of balances, referral abuse, fake deposit claims, exploit attempts, or interference with company systems may result in immediate account termination, transaction reversal, and legal enforcement where applicable.

6. Privacy & Data Use

User registration and compliance acceptance records are securely stored in company systems for operational, legal, and account integrity purposes.

7. Policy Updates

CRYPTO HUB GROWTH INVESTMENT reserves the right to update laws, operational policies, compliance requirements, and financial procedures at any time to protect platform integrity.
